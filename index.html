Executive Summary

Closing the gap between capability and opportunity for SMMEs

Grand Arbre Consulting is a specialised audit, compliance, tender support, and virtual administration firm dedicated to supporting Small, Medium and Micro Enterprises (SMMEs) across South Africa. The firm was established in response to a persistent and systemic problem within the SMME ecosystem: capable businesses consistently fail to access opportunities not because they lack technical ability, but because they are administratively unprepared, noncompliant, or poorly documented.

Grand Arbre Consulting exists to close this gap.
