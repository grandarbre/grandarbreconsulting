Audit and Compliance Support Services

Compliance Philosophy

Compliance is not an event; it is a continuous process. Many SMMEs only address compliance when a tender or funding opportunity arises, often discovering expired or missing documents too late. Grand Arbre Consulting adopts a proactive compliance management approach that ensures clients remain compliant throughout the year.

Comprehensive Compliance Services

We provide comprehensive compliance support, including but not limited to:

Company Registrations

  • Company registrations and amendments
  • CIPC compliance and annual returns
  • Beneficial Ownership registration and updates

Tax Compliance

  • SARS eFiling registration and maintenance
  • Income tax, VAT, and PAYE compliance support
  • Tax Clearance Certificate (TCC) applications

Enterprise Compliance

  • BBBEE affidavits and certificates
  • CSD registration, updates, and maintenance
  • CIDB registration and grading support
  • COIDA registration and compliance
  • Industry-specific regulatory compliance

Our role includes document preparation, submission support, follow-ups with regulatory bodies, and compliance status tracking.

Tender Documentation and Pricing Support

Tender Readiness Approach

Tendering is one of the most significant growth channels for SMMEs, yet it is also one of the most administratively demanding. Disqualification often results from technical non-compliance rather than lack of capability. Grand Arbre Consulting supports clients across the tender lifecycle, from opportunity assessment to final submission.

Tender Documentation Support

Our tender services include:

  • Analysis of tender requirements and eligibility criteria
  • Compilation of mandatory compliance documents
  • Preparation of company profiles and capability statements
  • Completion of standard tender schedules and forms
  • Internal compliance checks prior to submission

Pricing and Costing Support

Accurate pricing is critical to tender success and business sustainability. Underpricing leads to operational losses, while overpricing reduces competitiveness.

We assist clients with:

  • Pricing models aligned to tender specifications
  • Cost build-ups based on direct and indirect costs
  • Margin analysis and sustainability checks
  • Review of pricing schedules for accuracy and compliance

Our pricing support is practical, realistic, and aligned with the client's operational capacity.

Virtual Administration Services

Rationale for Virtual Administration

Many SMMEs operate without permanent offices or administrative staff. Hiring full-time administrators is often financially unviable during early growth stages. Grand Arbre Consulting provides virtual administration services that deliver structure without fixed overheads.

Scope of Virtual Administration

Virtual administration services include:

Communication Management

  • Email and correspondence management
  • Liaison with suppliers, clients, and institutions

Document Management

  • Digital filing and record keeping
  • Preparation of quotations and invoices
  • Basic reporting and document preparation

Compliance Management

  • Compliance calendar management and reminders
  • General administrative support

This service enables business owners to focus on revenue-generating activities while ensuring administrative continuity.
