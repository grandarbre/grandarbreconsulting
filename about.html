About GRAND ARBRE CONSULTING

Professionalizing SMMEs through integrated compliance and administration solutions since 2017

Company Background & Rationale

The South African SMME sector plays a critical role in employment creation and economic participation. However, despite numerous opportunities in public and private procurement, many SMMEs remain excluded due to non-compliance, expired documents, weak administration, or incorrect tender submissions.

Grand Arbre Consulting was formed to address this reality from a practical, hands-on perspective. Rather than offering fragmented services, the firm provides an integrated compliance and administration solution tailored to the real operating conditions of SMMEs.

Many entrepreneurs operate without physical offices, formal administrative staff, or structured systems. Others are technically strong but administratively overwhelmed. Grand Arbre Consulting positions itself as an outsourced compliance and administration department, allowing business owners to focus on execution and growth while compliance and documentation are professionally managed.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a leading compliance, tender readiness, and virtual administration partner for SMMEs, contributing to sustainable enterprise growth, formalisation, and long-term participation in the mainstream economy.

Our Mission

To simplify compliance, strengthen administrative systems, and professionalise business documentation for SMMEs through reliable, accurate, and affordable support services that enhance credibility and access to opportunities.

Our Offices

Strategic locations for enhanced accessibility and service delivery

Reitz Park Office

Primary Operational & Client Engagement Centre

Strategic Purpose: To provide direct, hands-on compliance and administrative support to local SMMEs, particularly in agriculture, construction, services, and emerging enterprise sectors.

Operational Functions:

  • Client consultations and compliance diagnostics
  • Document verification, preparation, and submission
  • Onboarding for virtual administration services
  • Coordination of tender documentation and pricing support
Boitumelo Mall Office

High-Traffic Commercial Environment

Strategic Purpose: To improve accessibility for walk-in clients and informal businesses transitioning into formal operations, with increased brand visibility in a commercial hub.

Operational Functions:

  • Walk-in compliance and registration services
  • Submission and collection of compliance documents
  • Administrative support and client follow-ups
  • Virtual administration onboarding and support

Integrated Location Strategy

Both offices are supported by centralised digital systems, enabling secure document sharing, real-time compliance tracking, service continuity across locations, and consistent client experience regardless of physical point of contact.
